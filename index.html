How to Delete Your Account and Data

Stillo is designed to work without requiring account creation. However, if you have used OAuth login (such as Google Sign-In) or want to ensure all your data is removed, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Request Account and Data Deletion

Step 1: Delete Local App Data

Since Stillo stores all data locally on your device, you can delete all your app data by:

Option A: Uninstall the App

  1. Go to your device's Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps or Application Manager
  3. Find and select "Stillo"
  4. Tap "Uninstall"
  5. This will permanently delete all locally stored data, including:
    • Focus session history
    • App preferences and settings
    • Timer configurations
    • All other app-related data on your device

Option B: Clear App Data (Alternative)

If you want to keep the app but delete data:

  1. Go to Settings → Apps → Stillo
  2. Tap "Storage" or "App Data"
  3. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
  4. Confirm the action

Step 2: Revoke OAuth Access (If Applicable)

If you used OAuth login (e.g., Google Sign-In) with Stillo:

For Google Sign-In:

  1. Visit Google Account Settings
  2. Find "Stillo" in the list of apps with access
  3. Click "Remove" or "Revoke Access"
  4. This will disconnect Stillo from your Google account

For Other OAuth Providers:

  1. Check your account settings with the respective provider
  2. Revoke access to Stillo from their security/permissions page

Step 3: Contact Us (Optional)

If you have concerns about data deletion or need assistance, you can contact us:

  • Email: stillo.aungthiha@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - Stillo

We will respond within 30 days and confirm the deletion of any data associated with your request.

What Data is Deleted

When you uninstall Stillo or clear app data, the following information is permanently deleted:

✅ Data Deleted Immediately:

Local App Data:

  • All focus session history and statistics
  • App preferences and settings
  • Timer configurations
  • Any cached data stored on your device

OAuth Information:

  • If you revoke OAuth access, we lose the ability to identify you through the OAuth provider
  • No login credentials are stored by Stillo (handled by third-party providers)

⚠️ Data That Cannot Be Deleted:

Anonymous Analytics Data:

  • Firebase Analytics collects anonymous, aggregated usage statistics
  • This data cannot be tied to your specific identity or device
  • Since the data is anonymous, we cannot identify or delete data associated with a specific user
  • This data is automatically retained for up to 14 months by Google Firebase for analytics purposes, after which it is automatically deleted

Crash Reports:

  • Firebase Crashlytics collects crash logs and error reports
  • These reports are anonymized and cannot be linked to your identity
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 90 days for bug-fixing purposes, after which they are automatically deleted

Why We Cannot Delete Anonymous Data:

  • Anonymous analytics and crash data do not contain personally identifiable information
  • We cannot identify which data belongs to a specific user
  • This data is aggregated and used only for app improvement and bug fixes

Data Retention Periods

Data Type Retention Period Can Be Deleted?
Local app data (sessions, settings) Until app uninstall ✅ Yes - Uninstall app
OAuth connection Until revoked ✅ Yes - Revoke access
Anonymous Firebase Analytics Up to 14 months ❌ No - Anonymous data
Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics) Up to 90 days ❌ No - Anonymous data

Important Notes

  1. No Account Required: Stillo does not require account creation. All data is stored locally on your device.
  2. Immediate Deletion: Uninstalling the app immediately and permanently deletes all local data. This action cannot be undone.
  3. Third-Party Services: Data collected by Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics) is anonymous and subject to Google's data retention policies. We cannot delete this anonymous data as it cannot be associated with your identity.
  4. Backup Data: If you have device backups enabled (e.g., Google Drive backup), app data may be included in those backups. You may need to delete backups separately if you want to remove all traces of Stillo data.
  5. Reinstallation: If you reinstall Stillo after deletion, it will start fresh with no previous data.

Verification

After completing the deletion steps:

  1. Verify Local Data Deletion:
    • Confirm the app has been uninstalled from your device
    • Check that no Stillo data remains in your device storage
  2. Verify OAuth Revocation:
    • Check your OAuth provider's permissions page
    • Confirm Stillo is no longer listed as having access
  3. Contact Confirmation (Optional):
    • If you contacted us, we will send a confirmation email within 30 days
    • This confirms we have processed your deletion request
